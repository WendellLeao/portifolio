About me

I've been working as a professional game developer for over two years using the Unity3D graphics engine and the C# programming language. I'm currently working on a team of Brazilian developers working as a Netcode programmer on the game Rogue Masters. In addition to this project, I've been working alone on a cooperative horror game for up to 4 people.

Rogue Masters

Rogue Masters is a 6 player co-op horde survival game with souls-like combat and extensive character customization where you fight Entropy in long-lost civilizations.

Horror

Horror is a 4 player online co-op horror game. Your objective is to investigate a haunted house and exorcism the ghost that haunts it.

Origens

Origens is a 2D side-scrolling game where the main character travels through a newly created world that geologically evolves through the ages. The player switches between two characters, but can only control one at a time. The characters have different powers and the player must combine them to be able to overcome challenges and finish the game. in long-lost civilizations.

Moto Simulador

Play and have fun with the most realistic motorcycle simulator and accelerator! Choose your bike, your engine and the track and accelerate using the handle or turning your phone like a real motorcycle!

Arrow is Cool in the Cemetery Skull

You are in a graveyard and you have to shoot arrows at skeletons to survive as long as possible.

Overthrow 2

In a dream-themed game, you must take down obstacles on your way to reach the end.
